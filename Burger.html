Torta de Milanesa

Components:

  • Bread: Telera
  • Premade Chicken Milanesa
  • Refried Beans
  • Crumbled Queso Fresco
  • Slices of Tomato (as many or little as you'd like)
  • Julianned lettuce
  • Fresh red onion rings
  • Picked jalapeños
  • avocado slices
  • Mexican Mayonaise with lime

Building it:

  1. Spread a thin layer of refried beans on the bottom half of the telera
  2. Place the chicken milanesa on top of the beans
  3. Layer the tomato slices on top of the milanesa
  4. Layer the lettuce on top of the tomato
  5. Layer the onion rings on top of the lettuce
  6. Layer the queso fresco on top of the onion rings
  7. Layer the avocado slices on top of the queso fresco
  8. Layer the picked jalapeños on top of the avocado
    • Make sure to spread them out evenly
    • Amount is based on taste
      • Some like it hot
      • Some like it mild
  9. Spread a thin layer of mayo on the top half of the telera
  10. Close the sandwich and press down

Serving it:

Once the sandwich is built, you can serve it whole or cut it in half. It's a big sandwich, so cutting it in half is recommended. It's a very versatile sandwich that can be served with many different sides, but traditionally it is eaten on it's own or if you think it won't be enough; make a second one =) .
